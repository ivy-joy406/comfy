FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I get a library card?

You can apply for a library card at any of our service desks. Just bring a valid photo ID and proof of address. The process takes about 5 minutes and your card will be issued immediately.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday-Friday from 9am to 9pm, and Saturday-Sunday from 10am to 6pm. Holiday hours may vary.

Can I renew my borrowed books online?

Yes! Log in to your account on our website or mobile app to renew items. Most items can be renewed up to 3 times unless another patron has placed a hold.

Do you offer home delivery services?

We currently offer home delivery for seniors and patrons with disabilities. Please contact our accessibility services department to learn more.
